About Qarabag FC

Qarabag FC is the most successful football club in Azerbaijan. Founded in 1951, the club has built a strong reputation through domestic success and consistent participation in European competitions.

Over the past decade, Qarabag FC has become a symbol of modern Azerbaijani football. The club is known for its tactical discipline, professional management, and long-term development strategy.

Qarabag FC represents not only a football team, but also national pride, carrying the Azerbaijani flag into European stadiums every season.
