About Qarabag FC

Qarabag FC is the most successful football club in Azerbaijan. Founded in 1951, the club is known for tactical discipline, modern football philosophy, and strong European experience.

UEFA Season 2025/26

The 2025/26 UEFA season is another important challenge for Qarabag FC. The team aims to compete with confidence and represent Azerbaijani football with pride on the European stage.

Team Identity

Qarabag FC combines experienced international players with talented local footballers, focusing on possession-based football and disciplined defending.

Club Ambitions

The club’s objectives include progressing in UEFA competitions, strengthening its European reputation, and developing young talent.
